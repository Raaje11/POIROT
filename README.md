# POIROT

> Evidence over opinion. Compliance before submission.

POIROT is an AI-assisted tender intelligence and proposal audit platform designed to uncover the issues that cause bids to fail before they reach the evaluator's desk.

Organizations invest hundreds of hours preparing responses to tenders, RFPs, RFQs, and procurement opportunities. Yet proposals are frequently rejected because of overlooked requirements, missing certifications, unsupported claims, eligibility failures, weak evidence, or internal contradictions hidden across hundreds of pages of documentation.

POIROT acts as a pre-submission investigator.

By analyzing both the **Tender** and the **Proposal**, it transforms unstructured documents into a structured compliance graph, extracts requirements, discovers supporting evidence, validates coverage, and generates an evidence-backed audit report that highlights submission risks before they become rejection reasons.

## Key Capabilities

### Requirement Intelligence

Extracts and classifies requirements across:

* Eligibility
* Financial
* Experience
* Certifications
* Staffing
* Infrastructure
* Legal
* Timeline
* Commercial
* Technical
* Submission
* Scoring

### Evidence Intelligence

Identifies and links proposal evidence directly to tender requirements.

### Compliance Validation

Determines whether requirements are:

* Satisfied
* Partially Satisfied
* Unsupported
* Missing
* Contradicted
* Unknown

### Deterministic Risk Scoring

Severity is calculated using deterministic formulas—not AI opinions.

Every finding receives:

* Severity
* Confidence
* Evidence References
* Remediation Guidance

### Red-Team Review

Assumes the proposal will be rejected and actively searches for weaknesses missed during primary analysis.
